This webpage provides information about clinics by Thousand Smiles Foundation, a Non-profit started by the Rotary Club in National City, CA. It serves financially challenged families in Mexico with an emphasis on care for children who suffer from a lack of dental care and maxillofacial deformities, such as cleft lip and palate. 

The data visualization includes data from our clinics starting in March 2021. Individual clinics can be slected via the drop down:

The blue line shows the average number of patients for all ages and all clinics. This is useful to compare individual clinics. 

Details are available by hovering over sections. The conditions shown reflect the patient’s history and may not reflect the particular reason for their visit. Also shown is the percentage of patients seen that reflect that condition and the numbers with that condition that are male and female.
